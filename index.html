History

The Sree (or Sri) community, often associated with the honorific "Sree" which means "revered" or "respected" in Sanskrit, hasroots in various Indian cultural and religious traditions . Members of this community are known for their contributions to Indian society, particularly in fields like spirituality, education, and social reform.

Reknowned Identities

Sree Padmanabhaswamy Temple (Ananthapadmanabhaswamy)

The Sree Padmanabhaswamy Temple, dedicated to Lord Vishnu, is a significant cultural and spiritual landmark in Thiruvananthapuram, Kerala. While not a person, it is central to the identity and history of the Sree community.

Sri Aurobindo

Sri Aurobindo was an Indian philosopher, yogi, and poet who played a significant role in the Indian independence movement. He later became a spiritual reformer, introducing his visions on human progress and spiritual evolution.

Sree Narayana Guru

Sree Narayana Guru was a prominent social reformer and spiritual leader from Kerala, India. He advocated for social equality, education, and the upliftment of the marginalized sections of society.
